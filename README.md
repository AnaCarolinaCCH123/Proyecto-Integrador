# Vehicle Guide 
Knowadays in the transportation industry there is a wide variety of vehicles with different characteristics. Imagine you are walking down the street and watch a vehicle and want to know more about it. That is why this program presents a compilation to help collect the information of different types of vehicles and help the user to know the specifications of the vehicle he or she is looking for, whether it is a motorcycle, truck, bus, car, or pick-up truck. The followinng program allows the user to enter the mame of a vehicle, and obtain all the information related to that vehicle. It is a practical data base for vehicles.
